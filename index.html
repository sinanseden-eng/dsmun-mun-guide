Writing a Resolution

(THIMUN Style)

Structure

A resolution is one long sentence. Preambulatory Clauses explain the "Why". Operative Clauses explain the "How".

Annotated Sample

FORUM: General Assembly
QUESTION OF: Nuclear Disarmament...
SUBMITTED BY: Japan
Header must match the agenda exactly.
THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY,

Noting the smuggling of illicit goods,

Further noting the need for action,
Preambulatory Clauses: Italicized starter, end with comma. No numbering.
1. Calls for Member States to sign the treaty;

2. Urges the creation of a monitoring body with:
a. monthly reports,
b. strict oversight;
3. Decides to remain seized on the matter.
Operative Clauses: Numbered, underlined starter, end with semicolon. Last clause ends with a period.
